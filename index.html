Pour Over

Clean, bright coffee with nuanced flavors.

Step 1: Medium grind 25g coffee beans
Step 2: Place filter and rinse with hot water
Step 3: Add grounds, bloom with 50ml water
Step 4: Pour slowly in circular motion
Step 5: Total brew time: 3-4 minutes

Espresso

Intense, concentrated coffee shot.

Step 1: Fine grind 18-20g coffee beans
Step 2: Distribute and tamp evenly
Step 3: Lock portafilter into machine
Step 4: Extract for 25-30 seconds
Step 5: Aim for 2oz golden crema shot

Cold Brew

Smooth, sweet coffee without bitterness.

Step 1: Coarse grind 100g coffee beans
Step 2: Add 1 liter cold water
Step 3: Stir to combine thoroughly
Step 4: Refrigerate for 12-24 hours
Step 5: Strain and dilute to taste
